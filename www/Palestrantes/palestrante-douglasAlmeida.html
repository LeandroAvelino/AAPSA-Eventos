Douglas Almeida

Director of Human Resources, Latin America - GE Healthcare

Douglas is the HR Director for GE Healthcare Latin America since April 2012.
He drives the strategic management of HR activities for the Latin America Region, which comprises over 1000 people with revenue of more than USD 1.0 Bi across multiple GEHC business segments and manufacturing plants.
In addition, he continues to develop and execute HR strategies that ensure maximum engagement and effectiveness of our human capital while partnering with the leadership team to build upon the team’s vision and strengthen the organizational structure.
Douglas is also the HR4HR Leader for GE Latin America since October 2013, which includes the HRLP Program Management.
He is responsible for the key initiatives for the development o f the HR team and also for building the function pipeline.

Douglas has over 24 years of experience in HR and has been working in GE Healthcare since 2008 when he joined us from GE Corporate as an HR Manager for Latin America.
He was promoted two years later into the Executive & Talent Development team as Organization & Staffing Leader for the region.
His tenure with GE dates back to 2001, when he was appointed Corporate HR Manager for Brazil.

For the past seven years, Douglas has led a number of important initiatives within GE Healthcare, such as the New Employee Assimilation events, which touched over 300 people throughout the region and the establishment of the Organization and Staffing team, focusing on the development of executive talent.
His achievements and contributions to the business and the Region resulted in being recognized with the Talent Champion Award by Mike Hanley, VP of HR, GE Healthcare Global.
